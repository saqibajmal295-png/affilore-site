Emergency Fund Guide: Building Your Safety Net

The Emergency Fund Target Planner helps you define your true bare-bones survival budget. Financial experts typically recommend 3 to 6 months of coverage, but for freelancers, 12 months is often safer.

Where to keep your fund?

Keep it liquid. High-Yield Savings Accounts (HYSA) are ideal as they offer safety and accessibility during a crisis.
