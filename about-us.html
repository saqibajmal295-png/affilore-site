About Affilore

Reliable, Instant, Client-Side Tools

Affilore is a curated web directory providing clean, lightweight, and completely free client-side utility tools for digital creators, developers, smart home operators, and home coordinators.

Our Philosophy: Serverless & Privacy-First

In a web filled with heavy tracking, paywalls, and slow backend response times, Affilore stands out. Every single tool in our directory runs 100% on the client side. This means:

  • Zero Data Transmission: Whatever inputs, values, or metrics you enter are processed entirely in your web browser. Nothing is ever sent to our servers.
  • Instant Performance: No server round-trips mean you get immediate calculations, sorting, and configurations.
  • Always Free: We do not charge subscription fees or require logins. You can launch and use our tools instantly.

Supported by Minimal Ads

To keep the lights on and support ongoing tool development, we display Google AdSense advertisements. We strictly coordinate our ads to be unobtrusive and non-disruptive, ensuring a premium user experience while keeping the directory completely open to the public.

Our Core Utilities

We build utilities that solve real mathematical, configuration, and layout problems. Whether you are matching room dimensions to clean air delivery rates (CADR), scaling culinary recipes, calculating smart device energy savings (ROI), or sorting complex skincare layered routines, our tools give you clean, objective, data-driven outputs in a single tap.

Contact & Support

Affilore is continuously expanding with new utilities. If you have tool suggestions or need assistance, feel free to use our directory resources. We are committed to maintaining the speed and integrity of this site for all users.
