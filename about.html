Our Mission

In a world overflowing with sponsored content and paid promotions, finding genuinely honest product advice has become nearly impossible. That's exactly why Affilore exists.

We believe every consumer deserves access to transparent, data-driven product reviews — free from brand bias. Our mission is simple: test real products, share real results, and help real people make smarter purchasing decisions.

The Affilore Difference

What Sets Us Apart

There's a reason thousands of readers trust our recommendations every month.

01

Hands-On Testing

We don't copy-paste spec sheets from manufacturer websites. Every single product we review goes through weeks of real-world testing in everyday conditions. If we haven't used it, we don't review it.

02

Data-Driven Ratings

Our scores aren't arbitrary. Each product is evaluated across weighted criteria — performance, build quality, value for money, and aggregated user feedback — to generate a transparent, reproducible score.

03

Zero Brand Sponsorships

We are not paid by any brand to review their products. Our revenue comes from affiliate commissions — meaning we only earn when you find a product genuinely worth buying. Our incentives are aligned with yours.

04

Regular Updates

Markets evolve, prices change, and new competitors launch. We regularly revisit and update our reviews and rankings to ensure you always get current, actionable information — not stale recommendations.

05

Brutally Honest

If a product doesn't live up to the hype, we'll tell you. If a budget option outperforms a premium one, we'll tell you that too. Every review includes a full breakdown of pros, cons, and who it's really for.

06

Community-First

Our content is shaped by what our readers actually want to know. From the categories we cover to the products we test — your questions and feedback directly influence our editorial calendar.

Our Process

How We Review Products

Every product on Affilore goes through our rigorous, multi-stage evaluation framework.

🔍

Research & Selection

We identify the most-searched, most-debated products in each category. We analyze search trends, community forums, and reader requests to decide which products deserve a deep-dive review.

📦

Acquisition & Unboxing

We purchase products at retail price (never accepting free review units from brands). This ensures our experience mirrors exactly what you'd get as a consumer.

🔬

Extended Real-World Testing

Each product is used daily for a minimum of 2–4 weeks. We test in real conditions — not lab environments — evaluating performance, durability, comfort, and overall user experience.

📊

Scoring & Comparison

Products are scored across standardized criteria. We use weighted scoring models and compare results against competing products in the same category to determine clear winners.

📝

Publication & Updates

We publish our findings with full transparency — including methodology, scoring breakdowns, and clear pros/cons. Reviews are periodically updated as prices change or new models launch.

How We Make Money

Affilore is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

When you click a product link on our site and make a purchase on Amazon, we earn a small commission at zero additional cost to you. This is the primary way we fund our operations, pay for products to test, and keep all our content free.

Importantly, affiliate commissions never influence our editorial decisions. A product that earns us a higher commission will receive the same honest treatment as any other. Our reputation depends on trust — and we take that seriously.
