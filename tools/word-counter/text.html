How to Use the Affilore Word Counter

Whether you are a student working on an essay, a blogger optimizing for SEO, or a professional writer drafting a report, tracking your word count is essential. This free online Word Counter tool is designed to provide real-time statistics on your writing, helping you stay productive and meet your goals.

Key Features

  • Accurate Word Count: Perfect for meeting strict submission requirements for assignments or articles.
  • Character Count: Essential for social media posts like X (Twitter) or crafting the perfect Google meta description.
  • Reading Time Estimator: Automatically calculates how long it will take the average person to read your text (based on 200 WPM).
  • Privacy Focused: Unlike other tools, your text is processed 100% in your browser. No data is sent to our servers.

Why is Word Count Important for SEO?

Search engines like Google prioritize high-quality, comprehensive content. Using the Affilore Word Counter helps you ensure your articles have enough depth to cover the topic thoroughly without "keyword stuffing."
