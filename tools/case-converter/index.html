Master Your Text with the Case Converter

Have you ever left Caps Lock on and typed an entire paragraph? Or maybe you need to format a list of blog titles correctly? The Affilore Case Converter is a free utility designed to solve these formatting headaches instantly.

Available Transformations

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capitals. Useful for headers or emphasis.
  • lowercase: Converts everything to small letters. Great for standardizing data inputs.
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every major word. Perfect for blog post titles and YouTube headlines.
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes only the first letter of the first word (and proper nouns). This is the standard for normal paragraph writing.
  • Inverse Case: Flips the case of every letter. Useful if you accidentally typed with Caps Lock on.
