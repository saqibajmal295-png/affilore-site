The Freelance Retainer Tracker: Stop Guessing Your Monthly Income

You closed the deal. The client signed the retainer agreement. Monthly recurring revenue — the golden words every freelancer dreams about — is now officially on the table.

Welcome to the freelance retainer trap: stable income, chaotic management. This free Freelance Retainer Tracker tool was built specifically to fix that. Below, we'll walk you through exactly why retainer management matters and how to build a business that runs like a real agency.

Why Retainer Clients Are the Backbone of a Sustainable Business

Project-based work is a treadmill. Retainer agreements flip that model entirely. Instead of trading one project for one payment, you trade ongoing, defined services for a fixed monthly fee. You get monthly recurring revenue (MRR) — the closest thing freelancing has to a salary that you control.

How Data Privacy Works

At Affilore, your financial privacy is our priority. This tool runs entirely in your browser using localStorage. This means your client names, rates, and hours are never sent to our servers. The data stays on your device, making it 100% private and secure.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a freelance retainer? A recurring contractual agreement where a client pays a fixed fee for defined services.

Do I lose my data? No. As long as you use the same browser, your data is saved automatically every time you make a change.
