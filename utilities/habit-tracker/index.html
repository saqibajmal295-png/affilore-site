The Seinfeld Strategy: Why "Don't Break the Chain" Works

When an aspiring comedian asked Jerry Seinfeld for advice on how to be a better comic, Seinfeld didn't talk about finding better material, studying crowds, or networking. He talked about a calendar.

He told the young comic to get a large wall calendar that has a whole year on one page and hang it prominently. Next, get a big red magic marker. For each day that you do your task of writing, put a big red X over that day. "After a few days you'll have a chain. Just keep at it and the chain will grow longer every day. You'll like seeing that chain, especially when you get a few weeks under your belt. Your only job next is to not break the chain."

Action Precedes Motivation

The mistake most people make when building a habit is waiting until they feel motivated to do it. The Affilore Minimalist Habit Tracker is designed to bypass motivation entirely. By gamifying the process of showing up, the tracker hacks your brain's reward system. You aren't writing a book today; you are just earning your green checkmark.

100% Private By Design

Your goals are your business. Unlike massive app ecosystems that harvest your daily routine data, require account signups, and push notifications to your phone, this tracker operates entirely on your local machine using standard HTML5 `localStorage`.
