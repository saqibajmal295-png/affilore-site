Why Client-Side Document Generation Matters

In the age of cloud computing, it has become normalized to upload our sensitive data to third-party servers just to perform basic file conversions. This creates unnecessary privacy risks, especially when dealing with client names, financial proposals, or proprietary business logic.

The Affilore Form to PDF Converter fundamentally changes this dynamic. By leveraging modern JavaScript libraries like html2pdf.js, the entire conversion process happens directly inside your local web browser. Your data is never transmitted over the internet, stored in a database, or saved in a temporary server cache.

Instant Preview, Zero Latency

Because the processing is handled by your device's CPU rather than a remote server, the live preview updates instantly as you type. Once you click download, the browser renders the DOM structure into a canvas, scales it to standard A4 dimensions, and serves the file directly to your downloads folder.
